Welcome to Froll.net

FROLL, developed on the BNB blockchain, is an innovative token with a total supply of 1 million, offering unique utilities and opportunities in decentralized lottery systems. Join the future of transparent, automated lottery experiences.

About FROLL

FROLL is currently used on the VIC blockchain at LotteryFroll.com for ticket payments and prizes in major global lotteries like Mega Millions, Powerball, and EuroMillions. On the BNB blockchain, FROLL powers an entirely new experience with automated lottery results. Both systems operate transparently and are governed by smart contracts, tailored to suit different user preferences.

FROLL Distribution

The entire supply of 1 million FROLL will be locked in a smart contract and sold to the community at a fixed rate of 1 FROLL = 0.039 BNB. If the total supply is not sold, the automated lottery system on Lottery.vin will not be launched, and all BNB will remain secure in the smart contract wallet, ensuring liquidity for FROLL buybacks at the fixed rate.

Benefits of Holding FROLL

FROLL holders enjoy exclusive benefits, including:

  • Profit Sharing: 10% of lottery ticket sales revenue will be distributed to FROLL holders on the 21st of each month based on wallet balances recorded at 0:00:00 UTC.
  • Fair Value: BNB in the smart contract wallet ensures buyback liquidity at the fixed rate.
